Where did you download the data (e.g., a web URL)?
We downloaded the data from Kaggle. 
[Kaggle] [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/dharmikdonga/academy-awards-dataset-oscars]

How was the data collected or generated? 
The data was collected independently, by various people who conducted profound research on the Oscars.
The source authors are Raphael Fontes and Dharmik Donga.

How many observations (rows) are in your data?
There are 10,396 rows.

How many features (columns) are in the data?
There are 10 columns.

What questions (from above) can be answered using the data in this dataset?
1) Do the Academy Awards reflect the racial diversity of films or are the #OscarsSoWhite?
2) How might certain historic, renowned ceremonies such as the Oscars discriminate against certain races?
